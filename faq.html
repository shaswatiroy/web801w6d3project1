Frequently Asked Questions


1 What age groups do you offer swimming lessons for?

We offer swimming lessons for children of all ages, starting from toddlers to teenagers.


2. How long is each swimming lesson?

Each swimming lesson is typically 45 minutes to 1 hour long, depending on the class level.


3. Are private lessons available?

Yes, we offer private swimming lessons for children who prefer one-on-one instruction. Please contact us for more information and availability.


4. What should my child bring to the swimming lesson?

Your child should bring a swimsuit, towel, goggles, and a swim cap if needed. Don't forget to apply sunscreen!


5. How can I register for a swimming lesson?

Your child should bring a swimsuit, towel, goggles, and a swim cap if needed. Don't forget to apply sunscreen!


6. What should my child bring to the swimming lesson?

You can register for a swimming lesson by filling out the registration form on our website or contacting us directly.


7. What safety measures do you have in place?

Safety is our top priority. We have certified lifeguards on duty during all lessons, and our instructors are trained in CPR and first aid.
